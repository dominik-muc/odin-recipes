Lasagna

Description

Tasty sauce and crunchy cheese on top of pasta? A perfect way to wind down after a long day. Who doesn't love lasagna?

Ingredients

  • Pasta (squares)
  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • Some spices of your choice, preferably basil

Steps

  1. Boil a litre of water and prepare the pasta. It should take around ten minutes for it to be ready to eat!
  2. While pasta is boiling, mash tomatoes with basil to make a sauce
  3. Put pasta and sauce into rectangle-shaped oven bowl. After putting some layers, put some slices of cheese on top
  4. Put it into oven 220 degrees for around 20 minutes
