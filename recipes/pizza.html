Pizza

Description

This is an easy recipe for making a mouth-watering home-made pizza!

With it's origin in italian kitchen, this simple dish took over the whole world. Children love it, adults love it, even my dog loves it! Why not make yourself a scrumptions meal today?

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • Some spices of your choice, preferably oregano

Steps

  1. Mix flour with water and some salt to make some dough. Then add some yeast with sugar.
  2. Next, let the dough sit for a moment while it grows
  3. Now spread dough across your table to make a circle-like shape
  4. Mash potatoes to make a sauce and put it on pizza dough
  5. Now add cheese and spices
  6. Put it into oven 220 degrees for around 15 minutes
